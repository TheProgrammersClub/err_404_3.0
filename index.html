What is ERR_404?

We call out all the cybernerds to come forth, compile your minds and execute your ideas in a competitive environment.
The hackathon will run across the weekend which offers students the chance to work in groups, brainstorm ideas, and build a working prototype before the time limit!
Whether it's your first hackathon, or your 50th, we would love to see students of all skill levels and from all colleges come along for a weekend of creativity, collaboration and coding.
Godspeed!

Rules and regulations!

Teams can be anywhere from one to five members. To ensure a level field for all contestants, all code, design and assets must be created during the duration of the Hackathon. You are, however, free to make plans, create wireframes, and brainstorm prior to the event. you are allowed to use 3rd party services, APIs, open source projects, libraries, and frameworks. All teams retain full ownership of what they have created during the Hackathon. There will be no alcohol,smoking allowed at the event. Behave professionally. Remember that harassment and racist, sexist, or exclusionary jokes are not appropriate for this event*. Attendees violating these rules may be asked to leave the Hackathon at the sole discretion of the Hackathon organizers. Thank you for helping make this a welcoming, friendly event for all.

    FAQ's


    • Who can participate?
      Anyone with a knack of programming and solving problems can participate.
    • How much does it cost?
      Thanks to our college, attendance is completely free. We will also provide you with WiFi, dinner and a workspace.
    • What should I bring?
      Bring your laptop, charger and other gadgets suitable to your project and a will to code. Please bring a valid ID for admission.
    • Where is it?
      M.H.Saboo Siddik College of Engineering, Byculla is happy to provide space in their campus to organize the Hackathon. You can find the directions in the Venue section.
    • Where will I sleep?
      We have sleeping area with matresses and pillows. You can bring along sleeping bags & other stuff.
    • I don't have a team :(
      Don't worry! Part of the fun of a hackathon is meeting new people. We will have time at the beginning of the event to socialize and form teams.
